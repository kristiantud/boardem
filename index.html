FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

"Minimal" - Does that mean there is less food on your boards

Short, but misleading answer, yes. By playing the crackers and breads separately, we can shift the focus of our presentation to showcase the essentials, the cured meats and artisan cheeses. A staisfying amount of food, just a minimalistic style of presentation.


The boards look so empty. Crackers and breads aside, there still has to be less food on them, right?

It may be surprising, but no. We use larger boards to allow space between each item. This prevents the melding of flavours and allows you to easily distinguish what you are enjoying, by taste and by sight.


Do we get to keep the boards?

We're sorry to say, you cannot. It's all a part of the presentation. The empty boards and wares would be picked up the following day. If you would like your own custom boards, please go through our artisans page (@boardem.co on Instagram) to find talented local craftsmen.


Can I still book a board last minute (less than three days notice)?

If it's possible, we would be happy to book you in. The reason we need at least three days notice is so that we can budget our time and expenses, and prevent food waste.
